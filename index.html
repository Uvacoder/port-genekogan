I am an artist and programmer interested in autonomous systems, collective intelligence, generative art, and computer science.

I code, give talks, teach workshops, write essays, and occasionally curate / organize.

I am interested in advancing scientific literacy through creativity and play, and building educational spaces which are as open and accessible as possible. My work is all free & open-source, and I record many of my lectures and tutorials.

  • Compiling a free educational toolkit on machine learning for art.
  • Advising at Runway ML.
  • Teaching at NYU.
